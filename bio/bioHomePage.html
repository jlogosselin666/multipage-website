A little bit about me... 3 truths & 2 lies

I was born in Montreal, Canada, in 1978. I first worked as a musician, having studied in several music schools in Canada, France and the Royal Academy of Music in London. In 2014, I discovered the joys of programming, and after much personal study I embarked on a 4-year degree at City University of London. I have since worked at Gamma Telecoms as a database analyst, at the Knowledge Academy as a skills trainer and more recently as an A-Level computer science teacher at Woodhouse College. I am now working as trainee software developer at IG Group, and I am loving it!

I am a black belt in karate.

I lived in Japan for 6 months.
